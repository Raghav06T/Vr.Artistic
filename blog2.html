Oil Paintings

Oil painting, painting in oil colours, a medium consisting of pigments suspended in drying oils. When a more flowing or mobile quality is required by the artist, a liquid painting medium such as pure gum turpentine must be mixed with it. In order to accelerate drying, a siccative, or liquid drier, is sometimes used.
