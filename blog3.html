Acrylic Paintings




Acrylic painting, painting executed in the medium of synthetic acrylic resins. Acrylics dry rapidly, serve as a vehicle for any kind of pigment, and are capable of giving both the transparent brilliance of watercolour and the density of oil paint. Easily clean up paint with soap and water while it's still wet. ​Acrylic paint becomes flexible and water resistant when dry.
